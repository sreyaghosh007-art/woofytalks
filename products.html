Why both exist

Different forms.
Same feeling.

The journals came first, born out of the small moments I wanted to remember about Sherry and the bigger ones I needed to process. Writing helped. So I built the journals I wished had existed on Day 1.

The nameplates came next. Because somewhere between the hundredth photo and the thousandth tail wag, I realised our home had fundamentally changed the day Sherry walked in. It deserved to say so on the door.

Both are handcrafted. Both come from the same place. Both are for you, if you're the kind of dog parent who feels everything.
