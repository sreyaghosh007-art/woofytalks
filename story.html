The story behind WoofyTalks

He didn't just find a home.
He chose his humans.

Sherry's story. Sreya's story. How one rescued indie dog changed absolutely everything.

Sreya Kaur and Sherry
founder and the real boss, Kolkata
4 years together

Sherry is wearing his favourite jacket. I am smiling because he finally stopped chewing the furniture. We are both very proud of this milestone.

  • December. An under-construction site in Kolkata.
    A malnourished mother. Seven puppies. And a heap of cement.
    A stray dog, too weak to feed her seven babies, covered them in loose cement at a construction site while she went to search for food. Then it rained heavily. The cement set solid. One puppy did not survive. The six who did were rescued just in time by a kind college girl who got them to safety.
  • A rescued puppy. A meeting. An unmistakable decision.
    I didn't choose Sherry. He chose me.
    When I met the rescued litter, I had no plan to bring a dog home. But Sherry walked right up to me, sat down, and looked me directly in the eyes. He didn't approach anyone else. He had already decided. I just said yes. From that moment, my whole world had a wagging tail in it.
  • Eight years of corporate life.
    I walked away from all of it. For him.
    I had spent 8 years building a career that felt less and less like mine. Sherry changed that. He showed me what actually mattered. So I quit. I stayed home. I became self-employed. And I discovered I was capable of things I had never imagined, because I had a dog cheering me on every single day.
  • Four years of learning the hard way.
    I made every mistake. Some were dangerous.
    First-time dog parenting is beautiful and terrifying. I made small mistakes and big ones. Some could have cost Sherry his life. I just don't want any other dog parent to go through that fear, that 3am panic, that crushing guilt. That is what WoofyTalks was built to prevent.
  • WoofyTalks is born.
    What would your dog say, if they could talk?
    That question is at the heart of everything I make. WoofyTalks is built around one idea: what if your dog could tell you exactly what they need? What they feel, what unsettles them, what lights them up. All dog talk. Straight from Sherry's heart to yours.
  • Right now. Every single day.
    Everything I do is for him.
    Every journal prompt. Every checklist. Every word on this website. It is my way of giving back to the dog who gave me everything. And my way of making sure every first-time dog parent feels seen, supported, and never alone.

"Sherry didn't just change my life. He showed me what my life was actually supposed to look like. Leaving corporate life for him was not a sacrifice. It was the best decision I have ever made. WoofyTalks is my love letter back to him, and to every dog parent who loves their dog the way I love mine."

Sreya Kaur, Founder, WoofyTalks, Kolkata
