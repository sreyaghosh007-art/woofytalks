Why these nameplates exist

He chose me first.
Everything else followed.

This is Sherry. He was rescued from a construction site as a puppy, covered in wet cement, barely alive. When I met the litter, I had no plan to bring a dog home. He walked up to me, sat down, and looked me directly in the eyes. He did not approach anyone else. He had already decided. I just said yes.

Sherry is the reason I quit eight years of corporate life. He is the reason WoofyTalks exists. And he is the reason these nameplates exist. Because every home that has a dog in it is a different kind of home. It deserves to say so on the door.

Every nameplate is made by hand, inspired by him, for every dog who chose their human first.
