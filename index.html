The story behind WoofyTalks

He didn't just find a home.
He chose his humans.

In December, a malnourished stray mother buried seven puppies in cement at a construction site. It rained. The cement set. One puppy didn't survive. Six were rescued just in time by a kind college girl.

When I met the litter, Sherry walked right up to me, looked me in the eyes, and decided. I quit 8 years of corporate life for him. He showed me what I was capable of.

Everything I create is my love letter back to him. Read the full story
